About the Project

Risk Assessment and Lifestyle Guidance for Chronic Disease Prevention

This project is centered on delivering proactive healthcare solutions through AI-driven risk assessment and personalized lifestyle recommendations. By analyzing biometric, behavioral, and lifestyle data, we aim to identify early indicators of chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease. Our goal is to empower individuals with actionable insights to improve their overall health and reduce long-term medical risks.

Using advanced data analytics and machine learning, we assess multiple factors—from sleep and diet to genetics and physical activity—to provide a comprehensive health risk profile. The system also suggests tailored lifestyle interventions that support prevention and healthier living.
